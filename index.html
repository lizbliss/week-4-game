Star Wars Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four characters below. By clicking on a character you will add a specific amount of points to your current total.


You win the game by matching your current total to the random number, you lose the game if your current total goes above the random number.


The value of each character is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each character.
